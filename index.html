About

Standpoint is a patent pending smartphone case with a built-in stand that allows your phone to stand at any angle on its own. When you are finished using your device, the stand simply collapses into the back of the case and easily fits into your pocket.

The design allows the screen to be placed in either the landscape or portrait mode, and the hinges provide a stiff secure feel. After dozens of iterations and tests, we've finalized our design and we are ready to begin manufacturing. We'll be launching on Indiegogo shortly.

Fold when not in use

The case was designed to fold into a thin single layer to make the stand as inconspicuous as possible when not in use.

Adjust to any angle

The elevating hinges are constructed with high carbon steel allowing the phone to glide to any position in a smooth continuous motion. The design features a centre pin which allows the phone to rotate 360 degrees and clip in to place at 90 degree intervals for portrait and landscape views.
